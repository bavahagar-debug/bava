Privacy Policy for Shorts Daily Uploader App

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user data.

Information We Collect

The app accesses your Facebook Page only to publish videos or content that you explicitly authorize. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data.

How We Use Information

The app uses your Facebook Page access token solely to upload videos on your behalf. No information is stored on external servers.

Data Storage

No user data is stored. Everything happens directly through Meta's API.

Data Deletion

If you want to remove your data or revoke permissions, you can do so anytime from:
- Facebook → Settings → Business Integrations
